Toddler Playtime Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Toddler Playtime does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app is designed for children and families and does not include advertising, analytics, or account login features. All settings and purchases are handled on your device only.

  • No personal information is collected or transmitted.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are used.
  • In-app purchases are processed securely by Google Play; no payment information is received by the developer.
  • Parents or guardians may contact the developer for any questions or support.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at toddlersplaytimebkapp@gmail.com .

Effective date: 07/04/2026

This simple privacy policy is provided for transparency and compliance. No unnecessary data is collected from users.
