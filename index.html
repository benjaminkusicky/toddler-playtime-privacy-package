2. Data Collected

The app is designed to minimize data processing. Depending on your use, the following technical data may be processed:

  • Local app settings and preferences stored on-device (for example language, sound, gameplay configuration).
  • Local entitlement cache on-device, used to remember premium unlock status.
  • Purchase-related technical data exposed by Google Play Billing APIs on-device, such as product ID, purchase token, order reference fields available to the app, purchase state, acknowledgement state, and purchase timestamp.
  • Basic app diagnostics generated locally for reliability and fraud prevention, if enabled in build configuration.

No account login is required unless explicitly added in a future release. No advertising SDK is included unless explicitly added in a future release.

3. Legal Bases

  • Contract: to provide purchased premium functionality and restore purchases requested by the user.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure transactions, prevent abuse, maintain app integrity, and resolve support issues.
  • Consent: where required by law for optional diagnostics or optional data uses introduced later.

4. Billing Data Handling (Google Play Billing)

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play. Google determines and processes payment instrument data under its own privacy terms. We do not receive or store full payment card details in this app.

In this Variant A model, purchase tokens and entitlement state are handled on-device for unlock and restore logic. The app may read Billing API responses from Google Play on the device but does not transmit purchase tokens to a developer backend in this variant.

Google may act as an independent controller for payment processing and store operations. Where Google provides services to developers, role allocation may vary by processing context and applicable law.

5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google Play services: required to complete billing, restore purchases, and enforce platform policies.
  • No additional third-party analytics or ads providers are used unless explicitly introduced and disclosed in an updated policy.

6. Children and Families Compliance

Toddler Playtime is designed for children and families. We apply data minimization and avoid behaviorally targeted advertising in child-directed context. The app does not request unnecessary personal data from children.

Parents or guardians may contact REPLACE_WITH_MY_EMAIL to request review or deletion of data that may be associated with support interactions.

7. Data Retention

  • On-device settings: retained until user clears app data, uninstalls the app, or changes settings.
  • On-device entitlement cache: retained for up to REPLACE_WITH_ENTITLEMENT_RETENTION_DAYS days since last app use, or until app data is cleared/uninstalled, whichever occurs first.
  • Purchase validation logs: not maintained server-side in Variant A. Temporary in-memory processing may occur during runtime and is discarded when no longer needed.

8. Security Controls

  • Use of Android app sandbox and platform storage protections.
  • Least-privilege design with no unnecessary permission access for billing.
  • Transport security for any network calls made by platform billing flows.
  • Internal access controls for any support mailbox or issue management system handling user requests.
